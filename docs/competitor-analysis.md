# Competitor Analysis
## Airbnb
### Strengths
* Large global user base
* Simple booking process
* Trusted reviews and ratings
* Good UI/UX
### Limitations
* High service charges
* Less focus on local eco-tourism
* Hosts depend heavily on platform policies
---
## Booking.com
### Strengths
* Large property collection
* Strong booking infrastructure
* Easy search and filtering
### Limitations
* Commission-based model
* Less personalized recommendations
* Limited AI-based planning
---
## MakeMyTrip
### Strengths
* Popular among Indian travelers
* Integrated travel services
* Good mobile experience
### Limitations
* Less focus on local homestays
* Third-party dependency
---
## Gap Identified
Current platforms mainly focus on large-scale tourism.
EcoStay Connect aims to:
* Promote local homestays
* Enable direct booking
* Reduce commission dependency
* Provide AI-powered recommendations
* Support eco-tourism growth
